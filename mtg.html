Magic the Gathering

Magic was first made in 1993, but new cards are released each year. It is a card game that you play against other players, using decks of cards that you each build separately.

I really enjoy watching and playing card games like Magic. I find the strategy aspect very interesting, the way the game forces you to think through your plays and plan ahead. There is a good deal of both skill expression through the order and way someone can play the cards, as well as personality.

Different decks can win in different ways. Some decks are fast and try to rush down your opponent, but can run out of steam quickly. Some decks are very slow, they aim to grind out a long game and stretch it to the point they can ultimately take over. Other decks can be placed somewhere in between, playing a few cards turn after turn that can be useful both in the early and late games.
